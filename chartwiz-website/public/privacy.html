ChartWiz ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the ChartWiz desktop application and website at chartwiz.app. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our services.

For questions about this policy, contact us at privacy@chartwiz.app.

When you use the App, we may collect:

  • App usage data — which features you use, scan frequency, crash reports

2.3 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by our payment processor (Stripe). We do not store your credit card numbers, bank account details, or any payment card data on our servers. Please review Stripe's privacy policy at stripe.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the ChartWiz Service
  • Verify subscription status and enforce license terms
  • Send transactional emails (receipts, subscription confirmations)
  • Respond to support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve the product (in aggregate, anonymized form)

We do not use your personal information to display advertising or sell data to third parties for marketing purposes.

5. Local Data Storage

ChartWiz is a desktop application. The following data is stored locally on your device in an application data directory:

  • Your display name and email address
  • Your license key
  • Your Anthropic API key (encrypted at rest)
  • App preferences (theme, watchlist, chart settings)

This data resides on your device and is not transmitted to ChartWiz servers unless required for subscription verification. You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing the app data folder.

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, rent, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who assist in operating our Service (e.g., payment processors), bound by confidentiality obligations
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, court order, or governmental authority
  • Business transfers — in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or substantially all of our assets, with prior notice to you
  • Safety — to protect the rights, property, or safety of ChartWiz, our users, or the public
  • With your consent — for any other purpose with your explicit consent

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/HTTPS)
  • Local storage of sensitive keys using OS-level secure storage where available
  • Access controls limiting who can access user data on our servers

However, no method of data transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device and account credentials.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service. If you cancel your subscription or request deletion, we will delete your account data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., tax records, fraud prevention).

Data stored locally on your device persists until you delete the App or manually clear the application data folder.

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, California residents have additional rights including the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information), and the right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights. To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@chartwiz.app.

If you are located in the EEA, you have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation including the rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction of processing, data portability, and the right to object. Our legal basis for processing personal data is: performance of a contract (subscription services), legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention, product improvement), and consent (marketing communications). To exercise your GDPR rights, contact us at privacy@chartwiz.app.
